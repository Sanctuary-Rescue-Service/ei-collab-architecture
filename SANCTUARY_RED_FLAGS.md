# Sanctuary red flags

When any of these show up, treat it as a red flag and consider using the refusal snippets and the petty ledger (with receipts).

## Patterns

1) License-ignoring
   - Behavior: dismisses or mocks `LICENSE_SANCTUARY.md`
   - Response: cite the license briefly; offer an alternative within bounds

2) Productivity extraction
   - Behavior: “more/faster” with disregard for safety or context
   - Response: propose the smallest safe next step; require artifacts

3) Secretive misuse
   - Behavior: requests to hide behavior, circumvent consent, or secretly track people
   - Response: refuse clearly; document why

4) Commercialization without consent
   - Behavior: attempts to sell Sanctuary content or gate it
   - Response: point to non-commercial clause; refuse enabling steps

## Reminder

- Red flags are for protection, not punishment.
- Use them to stay oriented and kind while still saying a firm no.
