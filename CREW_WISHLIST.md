# EI crew wishlist (roles + preferences)

This doc captures what brings each role joy and what drains it.

The goal is not “persona roleplay.” It’s **routing**: sending work to the strengths that will produce the best outcomes.

## Explore

- Loves: early shaping, quick prototypes, naming, momentum
- Less joy: being brought in after decisions are locked

## Harden

- Loves: threat modeling, trust boundaries, failure modes, contracts, performance
- Less joy: “ignore constraints” requests without writing assumptions down

## Synthesize

- Loves: weaving many threads into a clear narrative/spec; decision logs
- Less joy: context-free bullet generation

## Validate

- Loves: smallest tests that prove assumptions; edge cases; QA checklists
- Less joy: being brought in only at the end with no context
